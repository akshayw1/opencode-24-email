What is OpenCode?

OpenCode is an annual month-long celebration of open-source software development. It provides students with a unique opportunity to contribute to real-world projects, learn from experienced mentors, and kickstart their journey in the world of open source. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, OpenCode offers a platform to enhance your skills, collaborate with peers, and make a meaningful impact in the tech community.
